# Account & Data Deletion — Upcheck

> **Google Play requires a publicly reachable URL** describing how users delete their
> account and data, declared in Play Console → App content → **Data deletion**.
> Host this page (e.g. `https://upcheck.in/account-deletion`).

## Delete in‑app (recommended)
1. Open **Upcheck** and sign in.
2. Go to **More → Profile** (or **Settings → Profile**).
3. Tap **Delete Account** and confirm.

This permanently deletes:
- your **account** and authentication identity (email / Google / phone), and
- all **farm data you own** — farms, ponds, cycles, all logs (water quality, feed, sampling, mortality, chemical, plankton, microbiology, disease, treatments), harvests, inventory, expenses, transactions, tasks and simulations.

The action is **immediate and irreversible**. Backups containing residual copies are rotated out within **30 days**; records we must keep for legal/accounting reasons are retained only as long as required by law, then deleted.

## Request deletion without the app
If you cannot access the app, email **admin@upcheck.in** from your registered email address with the subject **"Delete my Upcheck account"**. We will verify ownership and complete deletion within **30 days**.

## What is kept, and for how long
| Data | On account deletion |
|---|---|
| Profile, account, auth identity | Deleted immediately |
| Farm/pond/crop & all operational logs you own | Deleted immediately (cascade) |
| Transactional email logs (Brevo) | Per provider retention, then purged |
| Encrypted backups | Rotated out within 30 days |
| Legally required financial records | Retained only as legally required |

Contact: **admin@upcheck.in** — Upcheck Technologies Private Limited.
